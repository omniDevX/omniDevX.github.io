Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2025

Vortex - Storm Chaser ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information
We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or contact details.

b. Location Data
We collect device location data using GPS to display accurate weather data and document storm events. This data is stored locally and is not shared with any third party.

c. Camera and Media
The app accesses your device's camera and photo library so you can document storms. All photos remain stored locally on your device and are not uploaded or shared externally.

d. Local Storage
We use a local SQLite database to store storm documentation (photos, time, GPS location). This data is never transmitted outside your device.

2. How We Use Information

We use your data only to support app functionality:

  • Display real-time weather based on your location
  • Allow you to capture and manage meteorological records
  • Improve user experience within the app

We do not use your data for advertising or share it with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud-based services that collect your data.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your locally stored data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You control your data. You can delete it anytime within the app or by uninstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. You will be notified of significant changes via an in-app message or update.
